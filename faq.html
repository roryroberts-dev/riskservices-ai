Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer a full range of professional services tailored to your needs. Visit our services page for a detailed breakdown, or contact us to discuss your specific requirements.

What are your opening hours?

We're open Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm, and Saturday 10am to 4pm. We're closed on Sundays and bank holidays.

How do I book an appointment?

You can book by calling us directly, sending us an email through our contact page, or dropping in during business hours. We aim to respond to all enquiries within 24 hours.

Yes, we offer free no-obligation quotes for all our services. Just get in touch with the details of what you need and we'll get back to you promptly.

What areas do you cover?

We serve customers across our local area and surrounding regions. Contact us to check if we cover your location.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, card payments (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfer, and contactless payments. Payment terms are discussed at the time of booking.
